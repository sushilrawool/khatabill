About KhataBill

KhataBill is a modern digital billing system designed specifically for small and medium businesses in India and worldwide. It simplifies billing, customer management, and sales tracking while reducing human errors and saving valuable time.

Why KhataBill?

Traditional billing using paper ledgers or spreadsheets can be inefficient and error-prone. KhataBill automates daily billing operations, tracks customer accounts, and generates insightful reports.

How It Works

  1. Add your customers and products into the system.
  2. Create bills quickly with automatic totals, taxes, and discounts.
  3. Monitor pending bills and mark them as paid.
  4. Generate detailed reports to analyze sales, customer activity, and product performance.
  5. Download reports in PDF or Excel for audits and accounting.

Benefits

  • Reduces errors in billing and accounts.
  • Saves time with automation and pre-calculation features.
  • Tracks customers’ payment history accurately.
  • Professional reporting for sales and performance analysis.
  • Easy to scale as your business grows.

Who Can Use KhataBill?

KhataBill is ideal for retail shops, grocery stores, small enterprises, service providers, and any business requiring efficient billing and customer management.
